Tools

A collection of browser-based utilities. No server, no dependencies — just open and use.

  • Base64 Encoder/Decoder — Encode and decode Base64 strings with full UTF-8 support
  • Word & Character Counter — Count words and characters with and without spaces
  • Wim Hof Breathing — Guided breathing exercise with sound feedback and streak tracking
  • Face Camera — Front camera with real-time face keypoint overlay
  • Scan Line Camera — Snapchat-style slit-scan: a line sweeps top to bottom and freezes each row as it passes
