Road Length in Polygon

Draw an area on the map to measure the total length of roads inside it (data from OpenStreetMap).

How to use: Click the polygon tool (▱) top-left of the map, click points to outline an area, then click the first point to close it. Roads inside are measured automatically. Click any road to inspect its OpenStreetMap tags.
